Georgia Energy: Hurricane Helene's Impact

Hurricane Helene has caused significant disruptions in energy services, leaving thousands of customers across Georgia without power.
Restoring Georgia Energy Services

Our dedicated teams are working tirelessly to restore power to affected regions. We have mobilized more than 20,000 personnel to ensure Georgia energy services are restored as swiftly as possible.

Current Outages in Georgia

Approximately 200,000 customers still remain without power. Our crews are prioritizing the restoration of Georgia energy services to critical areas and heavily impacted zones.

Progress and Timeline

We have successfully restored power to over 1.14 million customers, covering about 85% of those affected. Our goal is to fully restore Georgia energy to all customers in the shortest possible time.

Stay clear of downed power lines and flooded areas, as they may pose serious risks.

Current Power Outage Areas

We appreciate your patience during this time of energy restoration.

Check the Outage Map for the most up-to-date restoration schedules across Georgia.

Our priority is restoring power to critical areas first, including emergency facilities and densely populated regions.

We are addressing major challenges, such as debris-blocked roads and damaged infrastructure in Augusta, Savannah, and Macon.
